================================================================================
FLOOD RESPONSE PROCEDURES
================================================================================

During Floods:
1. Move to higher ground immediately
2. Avoid walking or driving through floodwater
3. Do not touch electrical equipment if wet
4. Evacuate if instructed by authorities
5. Contact emergency services: 112

After Floods:
1. Return home only when authorities say it's safe
2. Check for structural damage
3. Avoid contaminated water
4. Use bottled or boiled water for drinking
5. Report damage to local authorities

Flood Shelters:
- Temporary shelters are set up at community centers
- Bring essential items: ID, medications, important documents
- Food and water provided at shelters

================================================================================
EARTHQUAKE RESPONSE PROCEDURES
================================================================================

During Earthquake:
1. DROP to the ground
2. COVER your head and neck with your arms
3. HOLD ON to sturdy furniture
4. Stay away from windows, glass, and heavy objects
5. If outdoors, move to an open area away from buildings

After Earthquake:
1. Check for injuries
2. Evacuate building if there's structural damage
3. Avoid damaged buildings
4. Use stairs, not elevators
5. Be prepared for aftershocks

Earthquake Safety Zones:
- Open fields away from buildings
- Designated evacuation centers
- Strong, reinforced buildings

================================================================================
RESOURCE ALLOCATION
================================================================================

Food Distribution:
- Distribution centers in major cities
- Priority given to affected families
- Bring identification for verification
- Distribution times: 9 AM - 5 PM

Medical Aid:
- Mobile medical units in affected areas
- Hospitals on emergency alert
- Free treatment for disaster victims
- Medical supplies available at distribution points

Shelter:
- Temporary shelters at community centers
- Schools and public buildings used as shelters
- Bring your own bedding if possible
- Food and basic amenities provided

Water and Sanitation:
- Water distribution points set up
- Use only safe, treated water
- Report water contamination
- Sanitation facilities at shelters

================================================================================
EVACUATION PROCEDURES
================================================================================

When to Evacuate:
- Official evacuation order issued
- Immediate danger to life
- Building is unsafe
- Authorities recommend evacuation

Evacuation Checklist:
- Important documents (ID, insurance, etc.)
- Medications and medical records
- Emergency contact information
- Cash and credit cards
- Mobile phone and charger
- Change of clothes
- First aid kit

Evacuation Routes:
- Follow official evacuation routes
- Avoid blocked or damaged roads
- Use public transportation if available
- Help elderly and disabled neighbors

================================================================================
COMMUNICATION DURING DISASTERS
================================================================================

Stay Informed:
- Listen to official radio/TV broadcasts
- Follow official social media accounts
- Check government websites
- Avoid spreading unverified information

Report Emergencies:
- Call 112 for immediate emergencies
- Report damage to local authorities
- Share accurate information only
- Help coordinate rescue efforts

================================================================================
FIRST AID BASICS
================================================================================

Basic First Aid:
- Stop bleeding: Apply pressure with clean cloth
- Treat shock: Keep person calm and warm
- CPR: Only if trained
- Burns: Cool with water, don't use ice
- Fractures: Immobilize, don't move unnecessarily

Medical Emergencies:
- Call 115 for ambulance
- Provide clear location
- Describe the emergency
- Follow dispatcher instructions
- Stay with injured person until help arrives

================================================================================
IMPORTANT NOTES
================================================================================

- Always follow official instructions
- Do not spread rumors or unverified information
- Help neighbors and community members
- Keep emergency supplies ready
- Stay calm and organized
- Contact authorities for accurate information

For more information, contact:
- NDMA: www.ndma.gov.pk
- Local disaster management office
- Emergency services: 112
